Trie

Tries are very useful and are useful when implementing an autocorrect feature.

For exmaple, on the left we start with no letter. But then we branch of into the letters t, A, i. Let's focus on the t for now.

t branches off into to and te. to is its own word (for all that we know) so that's it for that specific word.

te branches off into tea, ted, ten


Lets understand that a bit, just in case:

te banches off into those words (child nodes) because, for all we know, those are the words that can be formulated using that te (parent node).
